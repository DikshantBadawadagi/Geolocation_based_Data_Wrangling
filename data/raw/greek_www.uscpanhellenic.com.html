    About Us

    USC PANHELLENIC

    USC's Panhellenic Council is the largest women's organization on campus. We are the governing council of 9 National Panhellenic Conference chapters. We are committed to creating a unified, multi-faceted community by empowering women to discover their fullest potential through continued community service, campus involvement, interfraternal collaboration, and academic dedication.

    Our Team

    The USC Panhellenic Council consists of fourteen executive board members and one to two voting delegates from each chapter. The Executive Board holds weekly meetings to discuss and develop initiatives for the betterment of the Greek community.
